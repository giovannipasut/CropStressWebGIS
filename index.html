FROM FIELD TO INSIGHT

Data Collection

High resolution multispectral and thermal imagery is collected using UAV surveys, together with ground observations and reference measurements to ensure reliable crop stress assessment.

Stress Analysis

Vegetation indices, thermal anomalies and spectral information are processed to identify crop stress patterns and quantify plant health across the field.

Explore Your Field

Visualize crop stress maps, compare different acquisition dates and inspect every area directly through the interactive WebGIS.

MyCrop is a WebGIS platform developed for multi-temporal crop stress analysis using UAV multispectral and thermal imagery. The platform integrates remote sensing, field observations and interactive spatial visualization to support precision agriculture.
